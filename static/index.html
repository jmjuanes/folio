FolioLite
Ideal for fast whiteboarding and single-focused tasks.
Privacy-First Storage
Folio Lite follows a privacy-first approach by relying on local storage for user data. Your work remains safe and local, ensuring your privacy is maintained.
Limited to a single board
Limited to a single board. Folio Lite encourages a focused approach, ensuring you concentrate on one task or project at a time.
Limited set of tools
Includes a subset of tools, streamlined for simplicity and ease of use. While it provides essential tools for drawing, it may not have the full range of options available in Folio.

Folio
A complete whiteboarding experience with support for multiple boards.
Privacy-First Storage
Folio also prioritizes user privacy by utilizing local storage for saving user data. This means your artwork and projects are securely stored on your device, enhancing data privacy and security.
Multiple boards
You can create and manage multiple boards in Folio, allowing you to organize your projects and artwork efficiently.
Comprehensive set of drawing tools
Folio provides the full set of drawing tools.
Libraries (coming soon)
Use libraries to store assets and custom drawings, and use them across your boards in Folio.
