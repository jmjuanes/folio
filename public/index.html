Does Folio save drawings on the server?
Nope! Folio values your privacy like a secret sketchbook. Your drawings are securely saved right on your device.
Do you use cookies or other tracking software on the application?
Absolutely not. Folio respects your digital privacy. No cookies, no tracking - just you and your canvas.
How can I share my drawing with my colleague?
Currently, the best way is to download your drawing as a .folio file. Then, simply share it with your colleague via email or your favorite cloud service. We are working on even more ways to share and collaborate in the future, so stay tuned for updates!
